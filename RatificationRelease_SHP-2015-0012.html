RATIFICATION OF ACTIONS ON SHORT PLAT SUBDIVISION SHP-2015-0012

AND RELEASE OF TRUSTEE



"Ratification and Release" means this document "Ratification of Actions on Short Plat Subdivision SHP-2015-0012 and Release of Trustee".



THE TRUST


The Oshien R. Pendell Trust (the "Trust") is a testamentary trust created in the will of Evelyn Knierim.

The sole beneficiary of the Trust is her grandson Oshien R. Pendell (the "Beneficiary"), who was a minor when the Trust was established.


When the Trust was established, its assets were a one-seventh (1/7th ) share of each of three parcels of real property in Spokane County, Washington State (the "Trust Assets").


The sole trustee is Daniel A. Knierim (the "Trustee").



THE SUBDIVISION


When the Trust was established, one of its assets was a share of a parcel having tax parcel number 45333.9097.

That parcel (the "Subdivided Parcel") has now been subdivided by the Short Plat Subdivision identified by the City of Spokane Valley as SHP-2015-0012 (the "Subdivision").

That Subdivision segregated the Subdivided Parcel into three new lots, with tax parcel numbers 45333.2101, 45333.2102, and 45333.2103.

A copy of the document specifying the Subdivision (the "Subdivision Document") is attached as "Exhibit A."


The Spokane County Auditorâ€™s office accepted the Subdivision Document into the public record on December 6, 2022, as document # 7256946.



THE SUBDIVISION ACTIONS


The Trustee took part in the subdivision and helped create the Subdivision Document.

The "Subdivision Actions" means everything the Trustee did for the subdivision and Subdivision Document.

One of those actions was signing the Acknowledgment section of the Subdivision Document as the representative of the Trust.


This Ratification and Release applies only to the actions described in this section.

It does not apply to any other actions by the Trustee.



NO EFFECT ON OWNERSHIP


This Ratification and Release cannot and shall not affect the ownership shares of any of the Trust Assets, regardless of whether the Beneficiary signs it or not.

The Trust will still own the same one-seventh share of the three lots in the Subdivided Parcel on behalf of the Beneficiary.



RATIFICATION


I, Oshien R. Pendell, approve of the Subdivision and Subdivision Document, and ratify all of the Subdivision Actions.



RELEASE


I, Oshien R. Pendell, also release and forever discharge the Trustee from any demands, or court or other legal action arising out of, or related to the Subdivision, Subdivision Actions, or Subdivision Document.
